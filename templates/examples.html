Interaction

Press the SPACE key to play or stop a sound.

Press UP ARROW to increase the speed of the sound.

Press DOWN ARROW to decrease the speed of the sound.

Press keys between 1 to 9 to select a single sound source from a mix of natural sounds. Not available for Highcharts.

Press ESC to reset selection. Not available for Highcharts.

Press m for meta data.
